# Project Presentation: EduCare Tunisia
**Exam: Python Web Programming (Django) 2025–2026**

---

## Slide 1: Introduction & Theme
- **Theme:** Tunisian Hope and Future for Children and Youth.
- **Project Name:** EduCare.
- **Vision:** "Build technology that protects and empowers the next generation."
- **Focus:** Early detection of school disengagement and youth distress.

## Slide 2: Problem Statement
- **Population:** Tunisian Middle/High Schoolers (Ages 12-18).
- **The Gap:** Disjointed communication between teachers (observers) and counselors (responders).
- **The Solution:** A unified digital workflow that converts behavioral observations and attendance data into actionable intervention cases.

## Slide 3: Architecture & Tech Stack
- **Framework:** Django 5.x (Python 3.11+).
- **Database:** SQLite (Scalable to PostgreSQL).
- **Frontend:** Bootstrap 5 with Enterprise-grade aesthetics.
- **Security:** Strict Role-Based Access Control (RBAC) with 3 roles: Teacher, Counselor, Principal.

## Slide 4: Functional Scenarios
- **Scenario 1:** Automated alerting. Teacher logs a flag -> Counselor receives an alert.
- **Scenario 2:** Follow-up adherence. Counselor tracks intervention -> System detects missed deadlines.
- **Scenario 3 (Advanced):** AI-Agent Protocol simulation for external resource acquisition.

## Slide 5: Advanced Tracks & Innovation
- **Robustness (Track D):** Model-level failure modeling (e.g., Closure Lock).
- **Privacy (Track B):** Shielded counselor notes; Principal only sees aggregates.
- **Integration (Track F):** Advanced Scraping with MCP (Model Context Protocol) and A2A (Agent-to-Agent) simulation.

## Slide 6: Evaluation Metrics
- **Workflow Completion Rate:** Share of cases resolved end-to-end.
- **Validation Pass Rate:** Success vs. Blocked actions in Audit Logs.
- **Security Coverage:** Verified by 6 automated RBAC test cases.

## Slide 7: Ethical Safeguards
- **100% Synthetic Data:** Zero PII or real medical records.
- **Anonymization:** Student names generated via Faker.
- **Human-in-the-loop:** System only alerts; counselors make the final decision.

## Slide 8: Conclusion
- EduCare demonstrates that software engineering can provide a rigorous, auditable, and compassionate support system for the next generation of Tunisians.
- **Definition of Done:** 100% fulfilled.
